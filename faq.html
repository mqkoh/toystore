FAQ's

What if I can't find what I am looking for?

 Not everything we carry in our store is in our online catalog. If you can not find something that you want, contact us by phone, fax, or email.

Is my order secure?

 Yes. The Dream Realm understands your concerns over Internet security. Because we share your concerns, we have a highly secure online ordering process - one that lets you shop and order with confidence.The result is a Web site that offers you the high level of service, convenience and trustworthiness our customers have come to expect.

Do you charge sales tax?

 We only charge you sales tax if you live in Malaysia.

Will you ship internationally?

 Yes, we love our international customers! We ship worldwide.

What is your return policy?

 We accept returns within 14 days of shipping. You will receive a full refund excluding shipping charges. All items must be unopened in their original packaging. If we made a mistake and shipped you the wrong item, we will pay for the shipping charges. Please email us for all concerns related to returning an item.
